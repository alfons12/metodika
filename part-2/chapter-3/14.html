Enumeration

An enumeration is a complete, ordered listing of all the items in a collection. For example list of order statuses (new, processed, delivered, cancelled). The reason why it is a separate artifact is that it can't be a domain entity as it is not a business entity and it can't be a Table (TBD link) because enumeratins are often modeled as a single table - all enumerations are stored in one table.

  1. Enumerations are neither domain entities nor database tables so Client is not an enumeration of clients it is an entity/table
  2. Enumerations don't have relationships to other enumerations. If you need it than you're not describing an enumeration.
