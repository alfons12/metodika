Artifacts

Describing stakeholder goals and capturing the functions the solution must have in text form is essential part of analysis in its early phases to get the initial idea of the problem. It also serves as a baseline for formal scope signoff in more formal development environments.

However, the textual requirements can't desribe every aspect of the problem such as user interface and ... These must be

Later on in the chapter talking about Effective analysis practices you will learn it's beneficial to work with the predefined set of artifacts during analysis and when documenting the systems. Having fixed artifacts enables team to standardize the analytical and documentation outputs throughout projects which improves readibility of the materials and simplifies onboarding of the new team members.

In this chapter we would like to introduce a couple of the artifacts as you need to at least have a rough idea what they represnt because they're mentioned in the next chapets. The full desripiton including also good practices and antipatterns for each aritfact are presented in the separate chapter.
