Artifacts

Artifacts are building blocks which form and describe solutions and their environment. They are sytem capabilities and constraints which the solution under analysis must meet, for example Requirement or Use case. They also describe the things in the problem domain, for example business rules and glossary terms. Finally, there are implementation-level artifacts desribing the actual parts of the system to be implemented or its outputs such as Screen, Table, Interface, Service, Function, or Document.

Artifacts are elements which represents aspects of the system, its requirements or constraints. The artifacts are analyzed and modeled during analysis and can be transformed to a system documentation after releasing the software.

As we mentioned manytimes the analytical outputs and system documentation should be as standardized as possible to enable easier knowledge transfer and readability.
